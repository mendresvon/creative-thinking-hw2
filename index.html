The Anatomy of a Device

An Exploration Through the Five Senses

Sight / 視覺

From a design perspective, sight is about a phone's identity. It's in the specific shade of its color, the curve of its corners, and its distinct shape. Think about how you can recognize an iPhone instantly just by the camera layout on its back. This visual branding is a key part of its design, making it recognizable even from a distance.

看手機的樣子，就知道它的設計。手機的顏色、樣子和角都很特別。比如，你看到手機後面的相機，就知道那是iPhone。這個設計很重要，所以你遠遠的看，也知道是什麼手機。

👂

Sound / 聽覺

Sound design gives a phone its voice. Every notification ping, ringtone, and alarm is carefully composed to convey a feeling—urgency, calmness, or excitement. These are not just random sounds; they are a form of audio art. The phone also lets us capture memories through voice recordings, but the designed sounds are what give the device its unique character.

手機的聲音都是設計好的。每個通知、電話和鬧鐘的聲音都不一樣，讓你覺得很急、很放鬆或很高興。這些聲音是一種藝術，不只是隨便的聲音。我們可以用手機錄音，記住一些事。但是，這些設計好的聲音，讓手機有自己的個性。

🖐️

Touch / 觸覺

Touch is about the physical connection to the device. Designers choose materials carefully—the coolness of glass, the sturdiness of aluminum, or the lightness of titanium—to create a specific feeling in your hand. The phone's weight, balance, and even the click of a button are all part of the tactile experience. Haptic feedback adds another layer, using precise vibrations to make digital interactions feel more real.

摸手機的時候，會有一種感覺。設計師選了不一樣的東西來做手機，像是玻璃、金屬。所以你拿著手機的時候，感覺會不一樣，有的很冰，有的很輕。手機的重量和按鈕，都是觸覺的一部分。手機也會震動 (Haptic feedback)，讓你覺得用手機跟真的一樣。

👃

Smell / 嗅覺

While the phone itself is odorless, our experience of it isn't. The first thing you notice is the "new tech smell" when you open the box. But the scent is also customized by our choices. A leather case adds a rich, earthy smell, completely different from the neutral scent of a silicone case. Smell becomes part of the personalization of the device.

手機自己沒有味道，但是我們可以聞到跟手機有關的味道。你買新手機的時候，打開盒子會聞到一個特別的味道。我們也可以自己選手機殼，皮的手機殼跟塑膠手機殼的味道就不一樣。所以，味道讓我們的手機變得很特別。

👄

Taste / 味覺

Taste is the most abstract sense, but phones influence it through curated design. Social media algorithms are designed to show you food that matches your "taste," making you crave certain flavors. Recommendation apps guide your choices for restaurants. In this way, the phone doesn't have a taste, but it acts as a designer of our culinary experiences, shaping what we desire and choose to eat.

我們不會吃手機，所以手機沒有味道。但是，手機App會給你看好吃的東西的照片，讓你很想吃。手機也會告訴你哪家餐廳很好吃。所以，手機雖然沒味道，但是它會影響我們想吃什麼，還有我們會選什麼東西來吃。
